\begin{abstract}
In this talk, I will illustrate left Kan extensions using filtered chain complexes and show how their decomposition into indecomposables can be used to compute vineyards. It will be a sort of lecture -1 of the seminar I will be teaching next semester, an intro/motivation to the topic.
